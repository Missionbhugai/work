About Me

Mission Bhugai

Hello! I’m Mission Bhugai, a 20‑year‑old civil engineering student at Western Regional Campus (Pachimanchal Campus), Pokhara.

By day I’m immersed in structural design and site planning; by night I’m a songwriter, producer and vocalist crafting original tracks. I also direct and edit my own videos, pen novels and collaborate behind the scenes with fellow artists—offering production, songwriting or vocal contributions.

This personal website is my creative hub: a place to explore my music, videos, lyrics and writing. If you’re an artist looking to team up or would like to license my work royalty‑free, drop me a line at missionbhugai13@gmail.com. For all other inquiries or just to say hello, stay tuned here for updates.

I built this site to share every new project and connect directly with fans and collaborators—thanks for stopping by!
